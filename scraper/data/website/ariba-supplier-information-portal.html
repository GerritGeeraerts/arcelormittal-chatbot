Ariba Project for ArcelorMittal

ArcelorMittal Europe - Flat Products is modernizing its procurement process! Our goal is to streamline communication, improve service, and make things easier for everyone. We are achieving this by using a digital platform called SAP Ariba®.

This website is your one-stop shop to learn everything you need to know about using SAP Ariba® as an ArcelorMittal supplier. This platform allows you to receive orders centrally, to send confirmations, ship notices and invoices in order to collaborate effectively with us. With over 4,000 suppliers already onboarded, we are moving towards a more digital and efficient future together.

To ensure a smooth transition and successful use of Ariba Network, a collection of helpful guides and links in various languages (Dutch, English, French, German, Polish, and Spanish) is provided below in a dedicated table on this webpage. To access the guides, simply click on the guide you need in order to download it locally to your computer.
