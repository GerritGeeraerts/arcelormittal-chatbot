Creating a low-carbon world – the case for a Carbon Border Adjustment

At ArcelorMittal, we are developing technologies to make low-carbon steel a reality, and these underpin ArcelorMittal Europe’s ambition to reduce carbon emissions by 30% by 2030 and to be carbon neutral by 2050. But we need the right legislation to make this change. That is why we are calling for a Carbon Border Adjustment that shares the burden fairly and supports all of us who are committed to reducing carbon emissions.

Read our manifesto on why we think a Carbon Border Adjustment is needed and how it could work.
